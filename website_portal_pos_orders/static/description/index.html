Customer Portal Point of Sales (POS)

Allow your customers to login in My Account Portal of your website and view Point of Sale Orders (POS Orders) and Print / Download POS Order receipts.

  • Allow your customers to login in the My Account Portal of your website to view POS Orders.

  • Point of Sales order(s) portal feature for customers in your website.
  • Allow your customers to Print/Download POS Order receipts from the portal website my account.
