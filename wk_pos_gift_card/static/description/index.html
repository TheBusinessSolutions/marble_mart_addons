POS Gift Card

Give customers a choice over regular gifts in POS!!

Odoo POS Gift Card module allows selling gift cards on Point Of Sale. It also allows selling multiple variants of the gift cards (based on price). Secret codes are created on sale of Gift Cards and those secret codes are provided to the customers. Multiple secret codes are created for multiple quantity purchases of a gift card.

Relieve your customers from the dilemma of buying products for others in Odoo!!

Buying products for gifting purposes is a time-taking and confusing process. Sometimes,it becomes impossible for customers to find a perfect product for gifting it to your loved ones in your POS store. This hustle of finding a good product eventually ends up in buying nothing from the store.

And customers not buying anything will not be acceptable for admin as it will lead to loss of sales and cart abandonment.

Provide gift cards to customers and increase your sales than usual!!

Now you can relieve your customers from the 'What to Buy' situation easily. Odoo POS Gift Card module enables your customers to create a gift card over the regular money for all occasions as per their pockets and offer good wishes. These gift cards are then used by customers to purchase their favorite products in store by using the unique code of the gift card.

It is in high demand now as customers can purchase the product as per their suitability. Also, you can offer them the choice of gift card for different prices customized by you from the Odoo backend.

Note: Odoo POS Gift Card Module works in conjunction with Odoo POS Coupons & Vouchers. You would need to install this module first for Odoo POS Gift Card to work properly.

Detailed Features List

Create A Gift Card In POS

  • The module allows you to create gift cards in POS.
  • These gift cards can be used as a present at the time of any occasion.

Generates A Unique Code Done For Each Gift Card.

  • Each gift card has a unique code associated with it for use in POS for payments.
  • Customers can purchase their favorite products in POS by using the unique code of the gift card.

Gifts Cards Can Be Printed And Used Physically At Store

  • Customers can create gift cards over hard cash as per their wish and use them as a present.
  • The gift card can be printed and used by the customer at POS to avail the discount.

Allows To Generate Different Variants Of Gift Cards

  • You can generate gift cards of different variants as per the occasion and based on the price.
  • Customers can generate gift cards for Christmas, Easter, Thanksgiving, Weddings, etc.

A New Voucher Is Created In Odoo For Every Sale Of POS Gift Card In Shop

  • A unique voucher is created in the backend for every sale of gift card.
  • Each voucher shows the unique code, redemption and other details associated with the gift card.
  • It allows for easy tracking and monitoring usage of sold gift cards.

Manage the gift cards from the Odoo backend

  • You can manage vouchers of all the sold gift cards in the backend.When a customer buys a gift card, it creates a corresponding voucher in the backend to keep a track of all the sold gift cards in store.
  • You can also keep a track of the gift card usage history under a whole different tab. It shows you the history of all the transactions made on the gift card by the customers.
  • When the customers use the gift card code, you can see the user history of the codes too.
