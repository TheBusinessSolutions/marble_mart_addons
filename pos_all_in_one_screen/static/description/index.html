Detailed Features List

Below is the list of features Odoo POS Advance Screens

Configuring Advance Screen

  • The module allows you to configure all three screens simultaneously in the same POS session.
  • Configure the Kichen, cart, and review screen with their unique URL and information.

Kitchen Screen in Odoo POS

  • Enhanced kitchen screen helps effortlessly manage the order in real-time.
  • The kitchen screen helps to get updates about every order from the kitchen screen.
  • Check the stage change of all orders and notify the chef about the order.

Customer Cart Screen

  • Add an attractive banner and title for the customer cart screen.
  • Updates the cart items in real-time while placing an order.
  • It shows a cart screen without any POS/IOT box.

Customer Review Screen

  • Add the desired title and image to make the review screen more appealing.
  • Allows you to get customer ratings and feedback.
  • View details of every review from the backend.

Launching the Screens

  • You can launch each screen separately at once using the URLs.
  • Each screen is accessible by the POS user, which they can launch as needed.

Check Desired Data from the Backend

  • You can access and check the kitchen, cart, and review screen data in the POS backend.
  • Also, you can see their details to get clarity about them.
The All-in-One POS Screen
POS Advance Screen permits you to use multiple POS screens using a single module. It merges the functionality of Odoo POS Kitchen, review, and cart screen into one single.
  • Kitchen screen allows you to efficiently manage the kitchen orders and attend to different orders efficiently so every order gets to the table on time.
  • Review screen enables your customers to rate their experience and provide ratings and feedback according to their experience.
  • Cart screen displays the product in a cart, making it easier for customers to verify what items they are purchasing and how much they cost.
POS Advance Search for Better Management
The module combines all three screen modules into one so you can avail all of their benefits. The module lets you manage the kitchen, cart, and review screen.
Additionally, you can configure each screen setting separately so everything is sorted according to your requirements. Also, having all screen data clubbed at a single location helps in the convenient and efficient management of them all.
Benefits of Odoo POS Advance Screen
  • The module streamlines the process of screen creation and order processing, reducing delays and enhancing the shopping or dining experience.
  • You can control all three screens from one place; hence, communication is more effective from the kitchen to customers.
  • You can customize the review and cart screens to promote the brand or store.
  • The review screen helps gather feedback that can aid in improving the services.
  • Custom URLs make it easier to launch screens easily and quickly for convenient access.

POS Advance Screen improves efficiency, communication, branding, and customer engagement, improving operations and customer satisfaction.
