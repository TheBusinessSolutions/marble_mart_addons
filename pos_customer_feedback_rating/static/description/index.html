Point of Sale POS Customer Feedback Rating - Odoo App

POS Customer Feedback and Ratings - Odoo App

This app allow your customer to gives rating and feedback of point of sales (POS) orders once customer paid for point of sale order. Your point of sales POS customer can give rating and feedback on purchase he/she made from you. Feedack and Rating request email will be send to your point of sales customer when he/she will pay point of sale order.

Main Features:

  • Allow your Point of sales (POS) Customer to give Feedback and Rating.

  • Customer will get email automatically when they will pay pos order.

  • Customer can also Unsubscribe from feedback and rating email if they do not want to get in future pos orders. If checkbox on customer form is ticked then only email will send to customer.
